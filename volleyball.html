Tennis Injury Prevention & Recovery

Fractures

Causes:

  • Impact from falls or collisions with equipment or surface
  • Being hit by the ball or racket
  • Falls onto hard courts
  • Twisting injuries
  • Overuse stress fractures

Preventions:

  • Wear protective gear if needed
  • Use proper technique
  • Ensure good court conditions
  • Wear appropriate footwear
  • Strengthen bones with diet and exercise

Recovery Methods:

  • Immobilization with casts or splints
  • Rest and avoiding stress on the fracture
  • Icing to reduce swelling
  • Physiotherapy
  • Surgical intervention if severe
Tendonitis

Causes:

  • Overuse of tendons in elbows, shoulders, or wrists
  • Repetitive swinging motions
  • Poor technique
  • Lack of warm-up
  • Sudden increase in activity intensity

Preventions:

  • Proper warm-up and stretching
  • Use correct technique
  • Gradually increase training intensity
  • Strengthen supporting muscles
  • Use appropriate equipment

Recovery Methods:

  • Rest and ice
  • Anti-inflammatory medications
  • Physiotherapy
  • Modify activity to reduce strain
  • Supportive braces or straps
Overuse Injuries

Causes:

  • Repetitive motions like swinging
  • Overtraining without adequate rest
  • Poor technique
  • Insufficient recovery time
  • Playing on hard surfaces repeatedly

Preventions:

  • Follow training plans with rest days
  • Proper technique and biomechanics
  • Strengthen supporting muscles
  • Use appropriate equipment
  • Listen to your body and avoid overexertion

Recovery Methods:

  • Rest and ice
  • Modify training routines
  • Physiotherapy
  • Gradual return to activity
  • Cross-training to reduce repetitive stress

About Sport

Volleyball is a dynamic team sport played by two teams of six players each. The goal is to score points by sending a ball over a net into the opponent’s court in such a way that they cannot return it. Players use their hands and arms to hit the ball, and the game emphasizes teamwork, agility, and strategic play. It is popular both recreationally and competitively worldwide, played indoors and on beaches.
